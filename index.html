Public Statement Regarding the FTP and Etc.

Sep 22nd, 2013

Okay since I have been asking several times about what is going on, I have decided to post a statement about our FTP service.

First a little back story…

There was a new #/g/tracker user/member who showed up around a month ago. This user/member was a very odd user who often brought up arguments on strange topics.

This member while a nuisance was tolerated as we are not an elitist group that silences people that annoy us.

Until this member, dropped dox on another member and attempted to on two other staff members. This is not tolerated in our humble community and he was banned. His /g/mail was also disabled in the process.

This made the now bannished member upset and he decided to report our FTP service to our hosting provider and to some copyright watchdog groups. Thus unleashing a huge shit storm upon /g/ and installgentoo.com community.

So the staff and myself went into containment mode, our primary irc channel was on lock down, the ftp was turned off and our main site was changed.

That is entire story, anything else was pure speculation or lie.


Today the staff has made some decisions on how things will be changed from now on.

  • First ftp access will be brought back shortly though with a new password, this password will rotate monthly and be posted in #/g/ftp on our irc.
  • We also plan on changing which web irc clients will be permitted to access our irc server, soon it will be just our own ran webirc client, sorry.
  • Our main site will return shortly, probably with some new design.
  • Some new more clear cut rules, apparently some do not know doxxing without permission is bad.



For now that is all, please if you have any questions you should now how to contact me.

Also if you really desire to see a real shit thread you can view that here, most of that thread is speculation and lies.  But hey you’re gonna believe what you’re gonna believe.

I am sorry for the downtime of our loyal users.
