0day Downtime -update2

Jun 16th, 2013

Okay another update, a little late, but hey.

Ramnode is still down and in owner is in the process of restoring all the node data.  This could be 24+ hours until done.  And this does not guarantee that ours had the data backed up.

I have gone ahead and setup our DNS elsewhere, on Amazon Web Services Route53.  This is resilient and not going anywhere.  I have also provisioned an Amazon EC2 instance to run our main site installgentoo.com.

By bring DNS back up a good portion of our services are back up as well, including img, ftp, gtracker, gtg, blogs, paste, books and probably another that I cannot recall at this moment.

Once this storm blows over I will look into possibly getting a better status site through amazon, as well as moving IRC over to amazon for better resiliency.

Anyway stay tuned hopefully tomorrow most of our nodes will be back up.

0day Downtime

Jun 16th, 2013

Okay so this is a big one.  Ramnode, our primary VPS provider had a massive 0day intrusion with their version of SolusVM panel.  This 0day leaked root passwords, and other sensitive info.

This brought down many of our services that run on ramnode, including DNS which once down made it diificult to get to the other IG services.  It would seem that the data is fine but we are waiting for the VPS to come back online.

More info can be read here:  https://gist.github.com/ElliotSpeck/66943b70c8b98e5b2afb

Our plan of action is this:

1.  Going to wait, I plan on waiting until this evening ~5hours to see if anything happens.  Ramnode comes back online etc. etc.

2.  If not, I will start buying new VPS to get us back online at this time.

 

Don’t forget our failback irc is IRC.P2P-IRC.NET @ #/g/tracker
