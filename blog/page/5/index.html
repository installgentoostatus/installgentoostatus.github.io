The Smoke Clears - 0day Downtime Conclusion

Jun 17th, 2013

Okay the craziness is somewhat over.  I have some good news and some bad news.

1.  /g/mail and news are dead, and I cannot restore from backups as I cannot find the key for the tarsnap archives for these services.

2.  Everything else is fine and back up.

So this means I will reinstate the mail server from fresh, and anyone with an account will need to ask again for one.  As well as the newznab service.

I am very sorry for this as I take full responsibility for botching the backups.

Going forward:


  • I will obviously be taking backups more seriously, starting with a new server for storing them in addition to offsite/offline backups.

  • Our DNS is now hosted with Amazon Web Services Route 53, no longer will all of our services go down if our primary DNS (was hosted with ramnode) goes down.

  • Since our DNS will be much more resilient I will be moving our status site and blog over to an Amazon EC2 instance, one will just have to remember status.installgentoo.com instead of the weird azure urls.


If there is anything I missed please contact me.

0day Downtime

Jun 16th, 2013

Okay so this is a big one.  Ramnode, our primary VPS provider had a massive 0day intrusion with their version of SolusVM panel.  This 0day leaked root passwords, and other sensitive info.

This brought down many of our services that run on ramnode, including DNS which once down made it diificult to get to the other IG services.  It would seem that the data is fine but we are waiting for the VPS to come back online.

More info can be read here:  https://gist.github.com/ElliotSpeck/66943b70c8b98e5b2afb

Our plan of action is this:

1.  Going to wait, I plan on waiting until this evening ~5hours to see if anything happens.  Ramnode comes back online etc. etc.

2.  If not, I will start buying new VPS to get us back online at this time.

 

Don’t forget our failback irc is IRC.P2P-IRC.NET @ #/g/tracker
