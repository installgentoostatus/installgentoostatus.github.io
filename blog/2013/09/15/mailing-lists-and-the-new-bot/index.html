Mailing Lists and the New Bot.

Sep 15th, 2013

Today I have successfully setup and added our own mailing lists server.  It is at http://lists.installgentoo.com.  Please sign up for our announcements list to stay up to date on news for installgentoo.com.  I have also added the mailing list lel, for general fun and just playing with mailing lists.

If anyone can think of other lists, or would like a list for their own project please let me know.

Also I need to mention that I have updated our beloved gtrackerbot, now gtrackerbot3.  It is a tweaked jenni/phenny bot that eventually I will repost the source for.  We are also currently tweaking and playing with a gazelle irc bot so that it can directly interface with our tracker.
