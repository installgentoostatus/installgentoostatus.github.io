Irc Move

Apr 29th, 2013

Everything is lined up for our official moving day.  May 1st, this coming Wednesday.  We will be leaving p2p-irc and migrating to irc.installgentoo.com; our own hosted irc server.

P2P was a great server but I think the time has come where we can really benefit from having our own IRC server.

 

So don’t forget, May 1st we are moving to irc.installgentoo.com
