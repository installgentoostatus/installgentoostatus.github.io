Jabber Service Is Back!

Apr 26th, 2013

The jabber/XMPP service is back.  Many moons ago this was used exclusively for staff to communicate, and went away after some time.  I have brought jabber back and opened for all to use.

 

http://jabber.installgentoo.com:9098/plugins/registration/sign-up.jsp

 

Is the link for registration.

To use, just add your username@jabber.installgentoo.com and the password you selected.  And then ask others for usernames or something.

Use it as an alternative to the deceased MSN?
