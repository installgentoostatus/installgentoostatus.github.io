Moving of the Status Blog.

Oct 13th, 2013

Our status blog has been moved an reworked countless times, and this is just another one of those times.

I have moved it to, yes, loltumblr.  For a few reasons…

First being I strongly feel that blogger is gonna get the axe one of these days, its stale, its stagnant, and just all around shit.  I can easily see google pulling the plug.

Next tumblr and newer blogging platforms have better integration with social media and other things. As well as just being all around newer, fresher and better feeling.

Some of you may be asking, like many others have, why I am not hosting my own blog.  Well if something happens, to say, all of the servers how would I let you know?

So for now we are on tumblr, I am silently contemplating moving to wordpress.com but tumblr is good enough for now.
