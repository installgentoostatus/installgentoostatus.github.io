Thanks to Some Long Time Staff Members.

Oct 14th, 2013

Today we say goodbye to two great and long time staff members of /g/tracker and other InstallGentoo services. dellfag and pictron have been with installgentoo since the early days of the tracker and have helped to build this community in to what it is today. Hanging the Staff Jacket up on the rack, they will be the first to take the Legend jacket off the wall and start wearing it with pride. Please stop in to this thread to wish them both well.
