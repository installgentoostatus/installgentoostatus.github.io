Update on the Ftp Server/issue

Jul 2nd, 2013

Okay first don’t be alarmed all of our precious data is safe.  We had a malfunction with the boot volume filesystem on the server.

After several failed attempts to fix (yay zfs), I have decided to just rsync all this shit to another (new, new, new?) server.  This server is much beefier than previous (again?) and eventually I will be moving gtracker and a few other services over to it (provided enough resources).  Anyway about another 24 hours of downtime I would say and everything will be back to normal.

Once the migration is complete and the ftp is back up I’ll post a few threads on /g/ (sigh).
